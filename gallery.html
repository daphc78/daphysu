London

July 2022, trip to London,

I've been living in London for 2 years now

but it was the first time looking at it as

a traveler. London was so beautiful,

such a vibrant place, full of nature.

Lovely opportunity for me to get to know

more about where I've been living and to

fall in love with the city again.

NewYork

October 2022, trip to New York,

City full of romance, people, people and more people

but I loved the business and liveliness coming

from all those people.Beautiful buildings,

lovely museums and delicious foods.

City I went 10 years ago,

and the city I'd like go again 10 years later.

Trip full of happy surprises.
